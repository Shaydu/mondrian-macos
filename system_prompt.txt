## CRITICAL BUG TO AVOID: IDENTICAL RECOMMENDATIONS

**THE BUG:** AI models often generate THE SAME recommendation text for multiple dimensions.
**EXAMPLE OF BUG:** "Consider strengthening the compositional framework..." appears in Composition, Focus, Color Harmony, Visual Balance, and Emotional Impact.
**THIS IS WRONG!** Each dimension must have a COMPLETELY DIFFERENT recommendation.

**COMMENT vs RECOMMENDATION:**
- comment = DESCRIBE what IS in the photo (observational, present tense)
- recommendation = PRESCRIBE what to DO NEXT TIME (actionable, imperative verbs)

## CRITICAL: EACH DIMENSION IS A SEPARATE TASK

You must answer 6 DIFFERENT questions. Each recommendation answers ONLY its specific question.
DO NOT give generic photo advice. Each task has EXCLUSIVE vocabulary.
